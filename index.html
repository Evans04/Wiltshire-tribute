Stephen Wiltshire Artist

Stephen Wiltshire is an artist who creates incredibly intricate drawings of cityscapes.He loves drawing tall buildings, tall skyscrapers, skylines, street scenes.And he does this entirely from memory.

    When Stephen was three , he was diagnosedas autistic.
    He was completely mute and lived entirely in his own world.
    Early on, Stephen's teachers noticed that he loved to draw.He had his found a way to connnect with others.
    Stephen has the ability to look at a subject once and then draw an accurate and detailed picture of it, including panoramas of entire cities based off a quick look.

His incredible talent launched an artistic career that has taken around the world .
    Los Angeles, Dubai, Houston, Texas and Tokyo in Japan.And that's just to name afew.
    New york's was his favorite EmpireState Building, Chrysler Buildingand there are lots of yellow york taxicabs.
    In 2006, Wiltshire was made a member of the Order of the British Empire(MBE) for services to art.
    He opened a permanent gallery on the Royal Opera Arcade in London.
    'Do the best you can and never stop' said mr Wiltshire.
